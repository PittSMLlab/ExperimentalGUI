# Weber Perception

Measures Weber's law perceptual discrimination thresholds for belt
speed asymmetry during split-belt treadmill walking. This is one of
Marcela Gonzalez-Rubio's doctoral thesis studies.

## Status

**Completed.** Data collection and processing are finished.

## Experimenters

| Role | Name |
|---|---|
| Lead | Marcela Gonzalez-Rubio |

## Paradigm

Participants make forced-choice perceptual judgments (which belt felt
faster?) at varying speed asymmetries to estimate the just-noticeable
difference (JND) in belt speed ratio. Responses are collected via Wii
remote.

**Instrumentation:** Bertec split-belt treadmill, Vicon motion
capture, Wii remote.
